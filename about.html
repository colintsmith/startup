Rock, Paper, Scissors... Shoot!®

The rules of rock, paper, scissors are simple! Choose any item to play and try to beat your opponent! Rock beats scissors, scissors cuts paper, and paper cover rocks. Come up with your own strategy and see if you can beat your opponent.

The game is thought to be originated in China during the Han dynasty. Instead of rock, paper, and scissors, the original options were snake, bird, or human.
